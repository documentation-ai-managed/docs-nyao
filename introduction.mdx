## Overview

Nyao helps you organize and manage all your project documentation in one centralized space. Create, collaborate, and publish docs effortlessly with an intuitive interface. Benefit from real-time editing, version history, and powerful search to keep your teams aligned and productive.

Whether you document APIs, user guides, or internal wikis, Nyao scales with your needs. Focus on content while Nyao handles organization, permissions, and deployment.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with Nyao in minutes.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit `https://www.nyao.io` and create a free account. No credit card required.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Workspace" icon="folder-plus">

    Set up your first workspace for your project. Name it and invite collaborators.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Doc" icon="file-plus">

    Click "New Doc" and start writing. Use the editor to format content.

```markdown
# My First Doc

Welcome to Nyao!

- Feature one
- Feature two
```

  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">

    Hit publish to generate a shareable link or custom domain.

  </Step>

</Steps>
