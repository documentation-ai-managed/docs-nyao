---
title: Cannot Edit a Document
description: Fix editing issues in Nyao documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is disabled
- You get a "Read only document" message

## Causes

- You have viewer role
- Document is locked by another editor

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Ask an admin to check your role in project settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document locked?">
    Wait until another user finishes editing before trying again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
