## Overview

Start creating documentation right inside your Nyao project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Select the project from the Nyao dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new doc">
    Click "+ New Document"
    Enter a title and start writing content in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click "Publish" to make your doc visible to the team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
